## Work — Wecommit

**[Wecommit](https://wecommit.ai)** builds AI execution automation tools for enterprises, public sector, and professional services. Not chatbots. Not demos. Systems that handle structured workflows, documents, and business-critical tasks with verifiable outputs.

### Products I actively own and ship

**[genDOC](https://gendoc.ai)** — AI Document Automation Platform

> Automates creation, review, and completion of complex structured business documents — grant proposals, government applications, corporate templates. Serves regulated workflows where output accuracy and format compliance are non-negotiable.
>
> One-click templates integrated with k-startup.go.kr and similar government portals. Format-preserving import/export for HWP, PDF, DOCX. RAG-powered knowledge base, AI review, version history, team collaboration, and secure distribution — all in one platform.

**[VoiceLead](https://voice.wecommit.ai)** — AI Lead Capture Platform

> Multi-modal lead capture for trade shows and enterprise sales teams. Speak, snap a business card, or scan a QR — Gemini AI extracts structured contact data in under 5 seconds. Built for Fortune 500s and global teams. Presented at CES 2026.
